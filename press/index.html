Description

Jarrah Technology is a one-person independent game development company based in Perth, Australia, specialising in strategy games. The name is a reference to the Jarrah tree, indigenous to southwest Australia.

History

Jarrah Technology started while Charles Cordingley lived in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Over those four years he created Concealed Intent, which unfortuantely did not sell well enough to fund fulltime game development. Moving to London, Charles worked in a startup for a few years before returning to his hometown in 2020 to try developing games again.
