Alternatives

I am becoming increasingly unimpressed with Javascript based gamedev. Perhaps it is time for trying another engine. In the meantime, I have been trying out Javascript for something it is very good at - websites. I reimplemented the SHD online feedback/stats engine using it in under a week. The tooling around JS is much better than I’m accustomed. Such a smooth process and it puts doing anything in Scala to shame. Now I am trying to create a tiny MVP web tool, it shouldn’t take too long. After that I’m going to try the Godot game engine. The buzz is that it has improved greatly over the last 6 months and my initial dismissal may no longer be valid. After that I just need to choose an engine and stick with it.
